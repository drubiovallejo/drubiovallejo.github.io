About

I'm a MS candidate and research assistant in computational linguistics at Brandeis University.

Previously, I completed a PhD in theoretical linguistics at the University of Delaware, where my advisor was Satoshi Tomioka. My doctoral research focused on the interface between the syntax, semantics and pragmatics of natural language, where I looked at the interaction between circumstantial modality, tense, and aspect.
